White hat hackers that is! Not the dreaded black hat cracker. White hat hackers help keep industries safe by hacking services (with explicitly written permission) in order to find security bugs; so the bad people cant use them to make them pay. White hat hackers do the following;

  • Find Security Flaws and Exploit them
  • Write About the Security Flaws Found and Hypothesizes on How Dangerous the Found Flaws Could Have Been.
  • Use This Information to Inform the Public About Said Exploits.
